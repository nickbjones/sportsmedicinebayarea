Bio: Matt Richardson enjoys working with all types of patients, particularly runners and skiers. He became board certified as an Orthopedic Clinical Specialist in 2012. His  philosophy is a balanced approach of manual physical therapy and targeted therapeutic exercises based on the best available research. He uses soft-tissue techniques for overuse injuries. Matt has completed three "Escape from Alcatraz" triathlons and swam in the 2011 ten-mile Trans-Tahoe relay. He enjoys hiking the higher elevations in the Sierra Nevada, fly fishing, and sailing. He received his doctor of physical therapy from Duke University and is a board-certified orthopedic clinical specialist.
