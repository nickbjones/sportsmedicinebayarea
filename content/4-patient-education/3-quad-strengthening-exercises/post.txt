### Self Help: Simple but Effective Home Quad Strengthening Exercises
by Richard L. Blake, DPM

Aphrodite, Athletic Trainer, Saint Francis Memorial Hospital, demonstrates for Dr Rich Blake's Blog entitled drblakeshealingsole simple, but effective, quadriceps (knee) strengthening exercises.
